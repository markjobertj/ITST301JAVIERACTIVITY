Services We Offer at Barangay Ayusan 1

At Barangay Ayusan 1, we are committed to providing essential services that improve the quality of life for all our residents. One of our key services is Health and Sanitation, where we offer free medical consultations and support through our Barangay Health Workers (BHWs). These workers assist with immunizations, maternal and child health services, and health education to promote wellness in our community. We also run sanitation programs aimed at maintaining clean public spaces and ensuring proper waste management across the barangay.

In terms of Educational Support, we strive to empower our youth and adults through various initiatives. We offer scholarships for deserving students, helping them pursue their education without financial worries. Our Adult Literacy Classes cater to individuals who want to enhance their reading, writing, and numeracy skills, while our Tutorial Services provide extra help for students struggling with their studies, ensuring they have the resources they need to succeed.

Our Social Welfare and Services programs are designed to support our most vulnerable residents. We provide regular aid to senior citizens through pension programs and other welfare initiatives. Additionally, we conduct community outreach programs, such as food distribution drives and assistance for underprivileged families. Our Livelihood Training programs aim to equip residents with skills that help them start small businesses or improve their employment prospects.

Ensuring the Safety and Security of our community is one of our top priorities. We maintain a Barangay Security Patrol that monitors and ensures peace and order within our locality. Our Fire Safety and Disaster Preparedness initiatives involve regular drills and training to prepare residents for emergencies. We also encourage community participation through a Neighborhood Watch Program, where locals help keep an eye on their surroundings to report suspicious activities.

We believe in the importance of Community Engagement and Recreation. Our sports programs offer opportunities for residents, especially the youth, to stay active and healthy through local leagues and competitions. We also organize Cultural Celebrations and Festivals, which bring the community together to celebrate our rich heritage and local talents. The Barangay Library and Learning Centers are open to all residents, providing access to books, educational materials, and computer facilities to support lifelong learning.

Lastly, we are dedicated to protecting and preserving our environment through various Environmental Programs. Our Tree Planting and Greening Initiatives encourage residents to participate in making our barangay greener and more sustainable. We promote Eco-Friendly Waste Disposal practices such as recycling and composting, and work on Water Supply and Management to ensure our community has access to clean and sufficient water.

These are just a few of the services we offer at Barangay Ayusan 1. We continue to work hard to build a better, more progressive, and caring community for everyone.
