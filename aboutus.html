ABOUT BARANGAY AYUSAN 1 TIAONG QUEZON

Barangay Ayusan 1 is a vibrant and dynamic community nestled in the heart of Tiaong, Quezon. Established as one of the barangays in Tiaong, Ayusan 1 is home to a population of over 2,700 residents (according to the 2020 Census), making it a significant part of the municipality. Our barangay has a rich history rooted in agricultural practices, local traditions, and community spirit.
